# Product Context

- **Purpose**: The Schweizer Fristenrechner is designed to assist users in calculating legal deadlines in Switzerland.
- **Problems Solved**: It simplifies the process of determining end dates for legal deadlines by considering weekends, holidays, and court recesses, which can be complex to calculate manually.
- **User Experience Goals**: The tool aims to be user-friendly, providing clear instructions and an intuitive interface for inputting dates and selecting deadline types.
